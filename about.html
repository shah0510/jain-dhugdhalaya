Who We Are?

Welcome to Jain Dugdhalaya, where we celebrate the essence of tradition and purity! Since our inception in 2000, we have been dedicated to crafting premium quality sweets and snacks that embody the true spirit of Jain culinary heritage. Our trademark, "Pashvam," symbolizes our commitment to authentic flavors and uncompromising quality.
As a pure vegetarian and Jain-friendly establishment, we offer an exquisite range of delights, including our signature basundi, Milk sweets ,indulgent dry fruit sweets, creamy shrikhand, and a variety of savory namkeen. We take pride in providing numerous options for our vegan customers, ensuring that everyone can enjoy our delectable offerings.
